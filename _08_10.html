執行模式

How It Works

  • 姐弟二人 + 專業美術老師團隊進入四川山區，連續駐紮 2 週
  • 每天利用孩子們的空餘時間教畫——尊重課業節奏，不打擾日常生活
  • 孩子們的畫帶回成都麓湖兒童中心公益義賣
  • 每幅畫定價 10–100 元（依尺寸 / 用紙 / 完成度）
  • 組織熱心公益群體全部買下，款項 100% 反哺貧困家庭
  • 媒體曝光由專業 PR 統一口徑
  • The sister and brother, together with a team of professional art instructors, enter the mountains of Sichuan and stay for two consecutive weeks.
  • Lessons run only during the children's free time — respecting their schoolwork and daily life, never interrupting them.
  • The finished works are brought back to Chengdu's Luxelakes Children's Centre and presented at a charity auction.
  • Each work is priced at RMB 10–100, by size, paper and finish.
  • A philanthropic buyers' circle acquires all works; 100% of proceeds are returned to the children's families.
  • All press is coordinated under unified PR direction.

為什麼是孩子的畫？小孩子的畫，大人模仿不了——童真、率性、未被規訓的觀看方式，本身就是最稀缺的內容。我們不修改、不"提升"，只把它鄭重地接到桌前。

Why a child's painting? A child's painting is something no adult can imitate — the innocence, the unguardedness, the unschooled way of seeing — and that, in itself, is the rarest content there is. We do not retouch them, we do not "improve" them. We simply receive them, with gravity, at the table.
