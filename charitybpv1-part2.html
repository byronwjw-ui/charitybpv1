《童年的回信》四川山區畫畫公益 + 麓湖兒童中心義賣 Letters Home from Childhood

The Third Thing — "Letters Home from Childhood"

項目名：《童年的回信》Letters Home from Childhood。每年 7 月（暑期）與 1 月（寒假）各駐紮 2 週，B.W 與 R.W 聯合，邀請專業高水準美術老師團隊同行，進入四川山區教孩子畫畫；畫作帶回成都麓湖兒童中心義賣，款項 100% 反哺對應家庭。姐弟與團隊全程不收取任何費用。首期：2026 年 7 月。

Project name: "Letters Home from Childhood". Two cycles every year — July (summer) and January (winter holiday) — each a two-week residency. B.W and R.W lead jointly, with a team of senior, professionally trained art instructors. The children's paintings are auctioned at Chengdu's Luxelakes Children's Centre; 100% of proceeds return to the corresponding families. The sister, the brother, and the team take no fees of any kind. First cycle: July 2026.
