09 · 第三件 ·《童年的回信》

《童年的回信》四川山區畫畫公益 + 麓湖兒童中心義賣 Letters Home from Childhood

The Third Thing — "Letters Home from Childhood"

項目名：《童年的回信》Letters Home from Childhood。每年 7 月（暑期）與 1 月（寒假）各駐紮 2 週。不是每期換一批新孩子——而是已加入的孩子每一期繼續學，同時每期新增一批新孩子與相應老師資源，已加入的家庭每半年持續獲得一次義賣反哺，規模逐期擴大。首期：2026 年 7 月。

Project name: "Letters Home from Childhood". Two cycles every year — July (summer) and January (winter holiday) — each a two-week residency. This is not a rotating one-shot programme. Each child who joins continues to learn in every subsequent cycle, while a new cohort and additional instructors are brought in alongside. Each enrolled family receives an auction-funded contribution every six months. The roster grows cycle by cycle. First cycle: July 2026.

運營模型 · 滾雪球式擴張

Operating Model — Rolling Cohort Expansion

三條核心原則

Three Core Principles

  • 已加入的孩子每一期繼續學。不是「教一次就走」，而是同一批孩子在後續每期都被持續教導，同一隻手由不同階段的老師接力陪伴。
  • 每期新增老師資源。隨累計學生數擴大，每期額外邀請 2–4 位專業美術老師加入團隊，保證師生比合理（目標 1 老師 : 6–8 學生）。
  • 已加入家庭每半年獲得一次義賣反哺。第一期加入的家庭，到第二期義賣依然會收到對應子女新作品的款項；累積三年後，每戶累計獲得 6 次反哺。
  • Every enrolled child continues in every subsequent cycle. This is not a "teach once and leave" model. The same children are taught again, cycle after cycle, by a relay of instructors at different stages.
  • Instructor team grows each cycle. As the cumulative roster expands, 2–4 additional senior art instructors join in each cycle to keep the ratio sound (target: one instructor for every 6–8 children).
  • Each enrolled family receives an auction return every six months. A family enrolled in the first cycle continues to receive auction proceeds from their child's new works in every following cycle. Over three years, each family receives six rounds of disbursement.

執行模式

How It Works

  • 姐弟二人 + 美術老師團隊進入四川山區，連續駐紮 2 週
  • 第一期老師：3–5 人 / 第二期：5–9 人 / 第三期：7–13 人——逐期擴編
  • 每天利用孩子們的空餘時間教畫——尊重課業節奏，不打擾日常生活
  • 畫作帶回成都麓湖兒童中心公益義賣
  • 每幅畫定價 10–100 元（依尺寸 / 用紙 / 完成度）
  • 款項 100% 反哺對應家庭，含本期新加入家庭與所有已加入家庭
  • 每期結束後出義賣明細與反哺台賬，所有家庭可查閱
  • 累計三年後輯為聯合署名雙語畫冊；單個孩子可被追蹤其六期作品的成長軌跡
  • The sister, the brother, and the instructor team enter the mountains of Sichuan and stay for two consecutive weeks
  • Cycle 1 instructors: 3–5 / Cycle 2: 5–9 / Cycle 3: 7–13 — progressively expanded
  • Lessons run only during the children's free time — respecting schoolwork and daily life
  • Finished works are auctioned at Chengdu's Luxelakes Children's Centre
  • Each work is priced at RMB 10–100, by size, paper and finish
  • 100% of proceeds returned to the corresponding families — including newly enrolled and all previously enrolled
  • Every cycle closes with an itemised auction ledger and a disbursement record, accessible to all families
  • Three years' work compiled into a co-authored bilingual album; each child's six-cycle artistic growth can be traced

為什麼是孩子的畫？小孩子的畫，大人模仿不了——童真、率性、未被規訓的觀看方式，本身就是最稀缺的內容。我們不修改、不"提升"。

Why a child's painting? A child's painting is something no adult can imitate — the innocence, the unguardedness, the unschooled way of seeing. We do not retouch them, we do not "improve" them.
