03 · ORIGIN

初衷：姐弟在同一張桌前坐下來的那天 How the Two of Us Came to This, Together

Origin: The Day the Two of Us Sat Down at the Same Table

這件事的起點不在董事會，也不在任何一份計劃書裡。它起於一次再普通不過的家庭通話——關於弟弟在加州的暑假怎麼安排，關於姐姐在四川剛落地的儲能項目，關於我們是不是該一起做點什麼。

This did not begin in a boardroom, nor in any planning document. It began with an ordinary family phone call — about how the brother would spend his summer in California, about the storage project the sister had just brought online in Sichuan, about whether the two of us should perhaps do something together.

姐姐 30 歲，已在新能源行業裡跑了八年。弟弟 16 歲，在加州讀高中，對塔羅、對 AI、對東西方為什麼想問同一些問題感興趣。我們發現：產業的這一端，姐姐能調動真實的資金、團隊與政府關係；文化與技術的那一端，弟弟能把一套東方的東西用世界聽得懂的語言重新講出來。我們各自缺的那一塊，恰好是對方手裡有的。

The sister is 30, eight years deep into the new-energy industry. The brother is 16, attending high school in California, drawn to Tarot, to AI, and to the question of why East and West so often arrive at the same questions by different paths. We came to a clear realisation: on the industrial side, the sister can mobilise real capital, real teams, real government relationships; on the cultural and technical side, the brother can take something deeply Eastern and re-tell it in a register the world can hear. What each of us was missing, the other already held.

於是我們決定不再分開做事。雪豹保護是姐姐主導的集團旗艦戰略，弟弟以青年觀察員身份參與；RBW Studio 是我們聯合創立的文化引擎，用最平民的價格給此刻焦慮的人遞上一份溫暖；四川山區的畫畫公益，是我們一起回到童年的家鄉，把熱錢換成山裡孩子的畫筆。三件事互不替代，但共用同一個底色：把中國西南、把香港、把世界，重新放在同一張桌前。

So we decided to stop working in parallel. Snow leopard conservation is the Group's flagship strategy, led by the sister, with the brother joining as a youth observer. RBW Studio is the cultural engine we co-founded — designed to offer warmth, at the most affordable price possible, to the anxious of our time. The painting initiative in rural Sichuan is the two of us returning to the home of our childhood, converting capital into paintbrushes in mountain children's hands. Three projects, none a substitute for the others, all sharing one ground: to place southwestern China, Hong Kong, and the wider world back at the same table.

B.W · 姐姐Sister

"我的基本盤是新能源、是碳匯、是青年企業家事務。但我希望這份基本盤能向外延伸——延伸到生態，延伸到文化，延伸到那些此刻睡不著、想找一個人說一句話的人。"

"My foundation is new energy, carbon assets, and youth-entrepreneur affairs. But I want that foundation to reach outward — into ecology, into culture, and toward those who can't sleep tonight and just want someone to say a single sentence to them."

R.W · 弟弟Brother

"我不是被姐姐帶進這件事的。我是和她一起想到的。技術、塔羅、雙語表達是我能拿出來的東西，山裡的孩子和雪豹的山，是我們倆一起要回去的地方。"

"I wasn't brought into this by my sister. We thought of it together. Technology, Tarot, bilingual writing — those are what I can bring to the table. The children of the mountains, and the mountains the snow leopards walk — those are places the two of us are returning to, together."

我們的願景樸素：在新能源產業之外，留下一份結果無關也站得住的長期資產——可被審計的公益履歷、可被引用的科研數據、可被翻譯的文化品牌、可被孩子們記住的一個夏天，以及可被一個普通人用一杯奶茶錢換來的一次溫柔回應。各自的長期理想抱負，姐姐在青年社會事務這條線上、弟弟在未來高等教育這條線上，都會自然兌現，不必在這份文件裡點名。

Our vision is modest: beyond the new-energy business, to leave behind a long-term asset that holds its ground regardless of any single outcome — an auditable record of charitable work, citable research data, a translatable cultural brand, a summer the children will remember, and one gentle reply a stranger can afford for the price of a cup of tea. Whatever longer-term aspirations each of us holds — the sister along the line of youth and social affairs, the brother along the line of future higher education — will arrive in their own time, and need not be named in this document.
