我們為什麼做這三件事 Why We're Doing These Three Things

Why We're Doing These Three Things

B.W 在新能源行業裡跑了八年。R.W 在加州讀高中。我們不替集團主業說不順耳的使命代言，也不為三件事接上同一個高大主題。我們只是認為這三件都值得做，才一起做。

B.W has been working in new energy for eight years. R.W is in high school in California. We are not borrowing the Group's industrial mission as a higher cause for the other two things, nor are we stitching all three into one elevated theme. We are doing them simply because we think each of the three is worth doing.

事實上三件事互相關聯

Where the three things actually connect

  • 地理：四川在三件事裡都出現——集團碳匯總部、《童年的回信》執行地；雪豹保護項目以三省區（新疆 / 西藏 / 四川）擇兩地試點，四川為候選之一。

🌿 零分成承諾機制（Zero-Profit Commitment）

🌿 Zero-Profit Commitment

本計劃若進入結果 A（碳匯可商業化開發），項目產生的全部碳匯收益由 WCS（50%）+ 當地政府（50%） 平分受益，定向用於雪豹棲息地保護與當地社區發展。四川天府中碳作為開發執行方零分成、不主張任何碳匯權益所有權，以免費技術服務方式持續承擔方法學、監測、核證、備案等開發流程工作。林權歸屬嚴格遵循《中華人民共和國森林法》及國家林草局相關規定。

If the project reaches Outcome A (commercially viable carbon assets), the entirety of carbon revenue is split WCS (50%) + local governments (50%), earmarked for snow leopard habitat protection and community development. Sichuan Tianfu Carbon, as the development executor, commits to zero profit share and claims no ownership of carbon rights, providing methodology, monitoring, verification and registration as free technical services. Forest rights follow the Forest Law of the PRC and the National Forestry and Grassland Administration.

本機制是 K6 媒體風險的最強結構性對冲，也是本計劃"結果無關的穩賺戰略投資最純粹形態"的核心依據。

This mechanism is the strongest structural hedge against media risk (K6) and the structural basis for the programme's classification as the purest form of a no-regret strategic investment.
